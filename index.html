Yotta · About the Tournament

A board, a clock, and everyone welcome.

YOTTA is hosting a Swiss-format chess tournament, played entirely online on Lichess, open to every player who loves the game — from weekend club regulars to first-time competitors. No eliminations, no byes for the unlucky: just a fair set of rounds where the board decides. All you need is a device, an internet connection, and a Lichess account.

How It Works

The Swiss Format

Instead of single elimination, every player is paired round after round against opponents with a similar score. Nobody is knocked out early — you play every round you're registered for, and your standing is built game by game.

  • Games are played at Blitz speed — 5 minutes per player, per game.
  • Everyone plays multiple rounds, regardless of result.
  • No direct elimination — a single loss doesn't end your tournament.
  • Pairings each round are based on current standings.
  • Final rankings are determined by total points.
